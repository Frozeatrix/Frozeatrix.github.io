Ratatouille

Philippe Sihabouth

Slide 1 - Objective 「目標」

It's the story of Remy who wanted to be a chef and Alfredo who is bad at cooking.

Slide 2 - Obstacle 「阻礙」

The problem is that Remy is a rat and can't cook in a kitchen.

Slide 3 - Make all-out Effort 「努力」

So Remy and Alfredo started helping each other. Remy would hide in Alfredo's hat so he could go to the kitchen.

Slide 4 - Result 「結果」

Thanks to their collaboration, the two have succeeded in making excellent dishes that have been recognized by culinary critics.

Slide 5 - Accident 「意外」

But the day where one of the world's greatest food critics comes, Alfredo decides to show the kitchen staff that he is working with Remy the rat. The kitchen team took this discovery badly and decides to leave, leaving Alfredo and Remy alone.

Slide 6 - Turn Around 「轉彎」

While Remy and Alfredo were alone in the kitchen, Remy's family, who were also rats, began to invade the kitchen and help the two characters serve one of the world's greatest food critics.

Slide 7 - Ending 「結局」

The food critic liked very much the dish made by Remy and Alfredo and decides to recognize Remy as a chef even if he is a rat.

The End
